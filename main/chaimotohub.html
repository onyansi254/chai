About Us

Welcome to Chai Moto Hub, where authentic Kenyan tea culture meets modern cafe elegance in the heart of Mombasa. Our cozy tea house is dedicated to serving the finest selection of Kenyan teas, each cup telling a story of our rich heritage and traditions. From our perfectly brewed traditional chai to our premium highland teas, every blend is carefully selected from Kenya's best tea gardens. Whether you're a tea connoisseur or just beginning your tea journey, we offer an authentic experience that celebrates the true essence of Kenyan hospitality. Discover the warmth of genuine Kenyan tea culture, where every sip connects you to our beautiful land and its people.
